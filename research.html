Research

My research interests include development economics, economics of education, political economy, environmental economics, and applied econometrics.

Higher Education Spillovers: How Private University Growth Reshapes Private School Quality

With José María Rentería · Under review at Economics of Education Review

This paper studies how the expansion of private universities affects nearby private secondary-school markets, with particular attention to changes in school quality.

Francisco Galarza and Jonatan Amaya (2025)

PEA Working Paper No. 211

This paper examines how roads and local institutions shape deforestation and spatial spillovers in the Peruvian Amazon.
